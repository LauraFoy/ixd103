    ABOUT ME

    Hi! My name is Laura and I’m an Interaction Design student at Belfast School of Art from Donegal. I am an aspiring designer who enjoys designing the things that could so easily end up looking dull and boring in a much more colourful, interesting and imaginative way. I love working on projects where I’m able to play and experiment with colour and I always try to infuse my personality, passions and somewhat eclectic influences into my work.
