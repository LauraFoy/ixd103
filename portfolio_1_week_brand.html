    • 1 WEEK BRAND PROJECT

    During the module on Exploring Identity. We were given the task of branding a Pizzaria of our choice (real or made up) in one week. For this I chose to take my favourite local pizza place and imagine that they are opening a new pizzeria-arcade hybrid location in my town, this way I could have a bit more fun with the task.
